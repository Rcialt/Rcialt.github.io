One County, Very Different Building Conditions

Grand County runs from 9,000-foot resort terrain in Winter Park and Fraser down to the open Colorado River valleys around Kremmling and Parshall. Snow loads, frost depths, water tables, and permitting all change with the terrain — and we've built in every corner of it.

Winter Park and Fraser mean alpine resort living and deep snow. Granby and Lake Granby are the gateway to Rocky Mountain National Park. Grand Lake is Colorado's largest natural lake with sensitive shoreline rules. Hot Sulphur Springs and the unincorporated county offer quieter mountain living, and the Kremmling valley suits ranches and riverside retreats.

Full-Scope Services, In-House

We're a custom home builder, licensed general contractor, and excavating contractor in one company. That means site work, grading, foundations, framing, finishes, and septic installation run on one schedule with one point of accountability.

We also handle additions and renovations — expanding a cabin near Grand Lake, updating systems in a Kremmling home, or adding outdoor living space anywhere in between.

  • New custom home construction engineered for altitude
  • Additions, renovations, and system upgrades
  • Excavation, grading, and septic system installation
  • Full project management with all permits handled in-house

We Know the County's Rules

Any construction, alteration, or demolition in Grand County requires a permit through Grand County Building and Sanitation Services, and most homes outside town limits run on septic — soil testing, engineered design, and state approvals included. Many subdivisions add HOA architectural review on top.

We file the permits, coordinate every inspection from foundation through final occupancy, and work with HOA committees so approvals don't stall your build. Local trades and suppliers across Fraser, Grand Lake, and Kremmling know us, which keeps materials moving and crews responsive.
