About us

From framing crew to full-service builder

Rondeau Construction started as a framing company in the late 1980s. Over 35 years, we grew into a full-service custom home builder, general contractor, and excavating contractor — because doing the whole job ourselves is the only way to guarantee it's done right.

We're family-owned and based in Granby. We handle everything from foundation to finish: dirt work, septic, framing, project management, and the final walkthrough. One team, one point of contact, no finger-pointing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of construction projects does Rondeau Construction handle in Granby?

We build custom homes, remodel existing ones, and manage full projects from start to finish. Our team also handles excavation, septic systems, fire mitigation, and flood restoration throughout Grand County.

How long does a custom home build typically take in Grand County?

Timeline depends on project scope, permitting, and site conditions — mountain sites and winter weather are real factors here. We'll give you a clear schedule during your initial consultation so you know exactly what to expect.

Do you handle remodels in Winter Park and Fraser?

Yes. We remodel homes and condos throughout Winter Park, Fraser, and the surrounding valley, and we manage the entire project so you don't have to.

Can you do the excavation and septic work too, or do I need a second contractor?

We own our own equipment and do our own dirt work — excavation, grading, utility trenching, and engineered septic systems. One contractor, one schedule, no waiting in line for subs.

What happens after a fire or flood damages my home?

We assess the damage, develop a restoration plan, and handle demolition through rebuild so you can get home safely. We worked extensively on Grand Lake area fire rebuilds and know the county's process.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Rondeau Construction is licensed and fully insured for residential construction, excavation, and septic installation in Grand County, Colorado.
