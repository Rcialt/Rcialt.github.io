Construction Project Management in Granby, CO

One dedicated project manager runs your build: permits, trades, budget, and inspections, with no finger-pointing between contractors.

What We Manage

Building a custom home in Granby means coordinating design, materials, labor, and inspections around mountain weather and permit checkpoints. We handle the logistics so you can focus on your vision.

From foundation pour to roofing, we make sure each trade arrives on schedule and the work meets standards. You get regular updates and direct contact with your project manager.

  • Planning and permit coordination
  • Trade scheduling and material tracking
  • Budget monitoring and change order review
  • Phase inspections and final walkthrough

Permits and Inspections

Granby building permits and inspections are essential checkpoints, not paperwork to rush through. We prepare the documentation, submit applications, and schedule inspections at framing, electrical, plumbing, and final stages.

We deal directly with inspectors and handle any corrections promptly. Familiarity with local code requirements means fewer delays and faster approvals.

Budget and Schedule Control

Surprises cost money. We establish a detailed budget before work starts and track every purchase order and invoice against it. Each change order is reviewed and approved with you before anything happens.

Scheduling is tight but realistic. We plan around Granby's weather, material lead times, and subcontractor availability, so the calendar reflects how mountain construction actually goes.

Local Experience Across Granby Communities

Whether your lot is in Granby Ranch, Grand Elk, or near downtown Granby, we know the landscape. Granby Ranch means master-planned community requirements; Grand Elk means HOA coordination; lakefront properties near Lake Granby bring their own foundation and water table challenges.

Our 35+ years of building experience — much of it right here in Grand County — means we have managed projects in all of these settings, and we know the inspectors, permit processes, and codes that apply to each.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does project management include on a custom home?

We oversee planning and permits, coordinate all trades and materials, track budget and schedule, run inspections at each phase, and manage communication between you and every contractor on the job. You have one dedicated contact for everything.

How do you prevent cost overruns?

A detailed budget is set before work starts, and every cost is tracked against it continuously. Change orders are reviewed and approved with you before implementation, so nothing lands on the final invoice unexplained.

How often will I hear about progress?

You receive regular updates from your project manager, and you can reach them directly. You always know what just happened, what is next, and when.

Can you manage projects in Granby Ranch or other HOA communities?

Yes. We work with Granby Ranch design guidelines, Grand Elk HOA standards, and the permit processes that layer on top of town and county requirements in master-planned communities.
