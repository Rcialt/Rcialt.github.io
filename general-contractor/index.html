What We Do as Your General Contractor

As a licensed general contractor, we take responsibility for the whole job, not just one trade. We pull permits, schedule and supervise subcontractors, order materials, coordinate inspections, and own the outcome. If something goes wrong, we fix it.

That covers a wide range of work in Granby: whole home remodels, structural repairs, fire mitigation, and rebuilding after fire or flood damage. You deal with one team and one point of contact from estimate to final walkthrough.

  • Permit acquisition and code compliance
  • Subcontractor coordination and scheduling
  • Budget tracking and change order management
  • Quality control and safety oversight

Remodeling and Renovation

From a kitchen upgrade to a full-house renovation, we manage the permits, the trades, and the schedule. Remodeling lets you stay in place while improving comfort and function, and we plan the work to keep daily disruption to a minimum.

Because we also frame, excavate, and pour foundations, we can handle the structural side of a remodel that many contractors have to sub out.

Fire Mitigation and Restoration

Wildfire risk in Grand County is real. Our fire mitigation work covers defensible space clearing, brush removal, and exterior hardening to reduce risk before a fire ever threatens your property.

If a fire has already done damage, we handle the restoration: assessment, demolition, decontamination, rebuild, and insurance coordination, all under our general contractor license.

Flood and Water Damage Restoration

Water damage from snowmelt, heavy rain, or pipe failure can wreck foundations, framing, and systems fast. We remove standing water, dry the structure, and rebuild what was lost, working quickly to stop mold before it starts.

We document everything for your insurance company and pull the Grand County permits required for structural, electrical, and mechanical repairs.

Why a Local General Contractor Matters

Granby's terrain, weather, and building codes require experience that only comes from working here long-term. We know the snow loads, the fire risk, the water management issues, and the local trades and suppliers who understand this region.

35+ years of building — including decades right here in the Granby area — means we have learned what works and what does not. That experience directly protects your investment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between your general contractor and custom home builder services?

Our custom home building service is for new homes built from the ground up. General contracting covers everything else: remodels, structural repairs, fire mitigation, and restoration after fire or flood. Both are run by the same licensed team.

Do you handle fire damage restoration in Granby?

Yes. We rebuild homes damaged by fire, working directly with insurance companies and managing the complete restoration from assessment through final inspection.

What does whole home remodeling include?

Major systems, finishes, and layouts throughout the home: kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, framing changes, roofing, or a full interior redesign. We scope it to your goals and budget.

Do you repair flood damage?

Yes. We extract water, dry affected areas, remove unsalvageable materials, rebuild damaged structure and systems, and work with your insurance to document the loss.

Do you offer fire mitigation for homes that have not been damaged?

Yes. We provide defensible space clearing, brush and debris removal, and exterior hardening to reduce wildfire risk around Grand County homes before fire season.
